Why Recoverly?

Turn hazy weeks into clear trends.

The first weeks after surgery blur together. Was today better than last Tuesday? Are tingling and spasms easing up, or just moving around?

Recoverly gives you a simple way to log each day and see those “I think so…” answers become clear charts.

  • ✔ Built around common lumbar and nerve symptoms: back pain, hip/glute pain, leg nerve pain, tingling, and spasms.
  • ✔ Track walking and sitting tolerance so you can see how activity changes week to week.
  • ✔ Designed to be used once a day in under a minute.

Understand your days

Use more than just your pain score.

Recoverly doesn’t try to replace your surgeon or physical therapist. It simply gives you a way to:

  • ✔ See which days felt like “overdoing it” and what you did beforehand.
  • ✔ Compare how back and nerve symptoms change versus last week.
  • ✔ Watch walking and sitting trends over time.

Share a clean report

Bring your care team into the picture.

When you’re ready for a follow-up, Recoverly can generate a clear PDF summary of your symptoms and activity to bring to your visit.

  • ✔ Weekly charts for pain, nerve symptoms, and activity.
  • ✔ Plain-language summaries to review together.
  • ✔ Designed for laminectomy, discectomy, fusion, and other lumbar procedures.
