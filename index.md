# Recoverly – Recovery Tracker

Recoverly is a simple, private iPhone app that helps you track back and nerve recovery after spine surgery or other lumbar issues.

> Recoverly does not provide medical advice or tell you what is “normal.” It’s a personal log to help you remember how you’ve been feeling and have clearer conversations with your care team.

---

## What Recoverly helps you track

- Lower back discomfort and aching
- Glute / hip discomfort
- Leg nerve pain or sciatica-style symptoms
- Tingling, numbness, and muscle spasms
- Walking and sitting tolerance
- Notes about flare-ups, good days, and days that felt like “too much”

---

## Key features

- **Today check-in**  
  Log pain, nerve symptoms, activity, and notes in under a minute.

- **History calendar**  
  See which days were mild, moderate, or severe at a glance using colored dots.

- **Weekly insights**  
  Plain-language trends comparing the last 7 days to the week before.

- **PDF reports**  
  Export a clean summary with charts to share at appointments.

- **Designed for spine recovery**  
  Built by a patient who went through lumbar surgery and wanted a clearer way to see progress over time.

---

## Privacy

- Your logs are stored on your device and in your iCloud backup if enabled.
- Recoverly does **not** create an online account.
- Recoverly does **not** sync your logs to any external server.

Read the full [Recoverly Privacy Policy](https://richr44.github.io/recoverly-privacy/).
