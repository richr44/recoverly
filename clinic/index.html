Recoverly — one-page patient handout

Shared as an optional patient resource.

I’m a Johns Creek resident who went through an L5/S1 microdiscectomy in October. I built Recoverly to help myself log lumbar symptoms day to day and notice patterns during recovery.

Recoverly does not provide medical advice. Data stays on the user’s device.
